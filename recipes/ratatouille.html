Simple Sheet Pan Ratatouille

Mice! Everywhere are mice!

wah wah wah wuh dream on dream on.

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 small green bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • cooking spray
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • salt

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Arrange eggplant, zucchini, bell pepper, and onion on a greased of foil-lined baking sheet. Spray vegetables with cooking spray. Season with 1/8 teaspoon black pepper.

  3. Step 3

    Roast in the preheated oven, stirring and rotating pan once cooking, until vegetables are lightly browned and tender, about 40 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Meanwhile, stir together tomato,oil , parsley, and 1/8 teaspoon each salt and black pepper in a large bowl. Add cooked vegetables anf remaining ¼ teaspoon salt. Toss to combine.
